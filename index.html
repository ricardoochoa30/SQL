Ejercicio 5

En que departamento hay más usuarios registrados

  • Departamento
  • ID
" SELECT departments.id, departments.name AS department_name, COUNT(users.id) AS user_count FROM departments JOIN users ON departments.id = users.departments_id GROUP BY departments.id, departments.name HAVING user_count = (SELECT MAX(user_count) FROM (SELECT COUNT(id) AS user_count FROM users GROUP BY departments_id) AS counts); "
